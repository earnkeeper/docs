# Why Subscribe?

Many of our services are free of charge, but to build a sustainable service we can run and maintain for years to come, we also offer a monthly subscription with the following benefits:

- An advert free experience
- Access to all metrics for all plugins
- Export all data to excel 
- Premium one on one support on discord

Your subscription keeps our team of 5+ people able to work on the platform full time. This allows us to provide a much higher quality experience than other game tools out there. We do this by:

- Working directly with the game developers for access and promotion
- Building and testing a consistent, powerful and mobile ready front end website
- Sourcing and rewarding of great developers out there who play the game and have already built tools by themself
- Constantly updating our platform to make it easier for developers to add to it, and a pleasure for gamers to use

And much much more!
